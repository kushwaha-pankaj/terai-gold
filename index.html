Our Vision

Bringing Nepal's golden purity to every home

In the fertile plains of Nepal's Terai region, where the Himalayas meet the Ganges, mustard seeds grow under pristine conditions. Our mission is to preserve the traditional cold-pressing methods that have been passed down for generations.

Every drop of Terai Gold carries the essence of this sacred land - pure, unrefined, and rich with the nutrients that nature intended. We work directly with local farmers, ensuring fair trade and sustainable practices that honor both the earth and our community.
